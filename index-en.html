Areas of Interest
    The group focuses on following areas of research:
    1. Video streaming over the IP protocol, including algorithms for video on demand, quality of experience, admission control, security, and performance evaluation. IPTV
    2. Multihoming/multipath systems and its application in the transmission of voice and video.
    3. SCTP Protocol.
    4. Strategies based on artificial intelligence, time series, Markov chains and other to solve problems in the area of communication networks.
    5. Communication systems for Smartgrid and IoT.
    6. Cognitive radio.
    7. Wireless and mobile Networks, including WIFI and LTE.
    8. Software Defined Networks (SDN), Network Function Virtualization (NFV), Openflow.

    We're always looking for talented people to join our team. If you wish to start a research career in one of above areas, contact us.
