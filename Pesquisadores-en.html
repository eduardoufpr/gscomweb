People
Research teams consist of professors researchers, doctorate students, master students and undergraduates.
From 2014 all the research teams of the line will be concentrated in the Communication Networks Laboratory. This lab will will bring together teams in one physical space, allowing the exchange of experiences among researchers, and sharing equipment acquired on projects.
